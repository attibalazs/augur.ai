Electrical Submersible Pumps (ESP)

The oil field uses several different types of artificial lift mechanisms to increase the production of a well. The ESP is a common form of artificial lift used throughtout the world. ESPs have a motor that is controlled from the surface that runs a multistage centrifugal pump. ESPs make up 20% of all artificial lift used in oil wells.
